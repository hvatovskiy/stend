Why does Somalia matter?

Somalia hopes to restore stability by electing a new president.

08 Feb 2017 19:54 GMT Politics, War & Conflict, Africa, Somalia

It was an unusual presidential election, with 22 candidates and no polling stations. MPs voted but not everyone else because of the threat of attack.

The election was marred by accusations of bribery and political infighting among the clans of Somalia, all 135 of them.

So will the new president be able to lead Somalis through the many political and security challenges?
