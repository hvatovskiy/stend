A bell-ringer is recovering after being dropped in what's been described as a "freak accident" at Worcester Cathedral.

The ringing master at the cathedral, Mark Regan, gave a vivid account to BBC Hereford and Worcester of the moment Ian Bowman was flipped upside down.

Mr Bowman was lowered 80ft (24m) through a trap door in the cathedral by a specialist rescue unit.

The accident happened during Evensong on Saturday when the bell-ringing rope caught Mr Bowman's heel.
