Officers were called to the Floating Harbour at about midday after reports a man's body was found under the bridge in Oxford Street.

Deakon Wilkins, 24, from Weston-super-Mare in Somerset, vanished after leaving Motion nightclub in the early hours of 14 January.

Formal identification has not yet taken place, but his family has been advised.

In a Facebook post, Deakon's father Andy wrote:

"Just to let people know the police have found my son Deakon's body today.
