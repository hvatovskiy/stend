A teenage girl who has been repeatedly groped while watching bands wants action against offenders.

Amy Baker, 17, from Southampton, who has waived her right to anonymity, says she has "had enough" of being sexually assaulted by men at gigs.

Harry Koisser from the band Peace says he has made sure their security team is fully aware of the issue.
