Vietnam's Tech Rush

Vietnam's young tech entrepreneurs are transforming the country into Southeast Asia's Silicon Valley.

19 Jan 2017 19:00 GMT Science & Technology, Vietnam, Business & Economy

Communist Vietnam's start-up scene is booming.

Young, highly skilled entrepreneurs are grabbing the attention - and cheque books - of international investors and turning the country into one of Southeast Asia's most exciting breeding grounds for tech innovation.

The country has big ambitions - the government wants to double the number of start-ups to one million in the next five years.

101 East explores how a new generation is rapidly transforming Vietnam from a Communist nation reliant on farming and manufacturing into Southeast Asia's Silicon Valley.
